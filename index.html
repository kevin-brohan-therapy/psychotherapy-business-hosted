About

At East Manchester Psychotherapy, we offer professional online psychotherapy to individuals across the UK. Our practice is founded on a deep respect for the complexity of human experience. We provide a safe, confidential space where all parts of you are welcome—whether they are painful, uncertain, hidden, or just beginning to take shape.

With integrity and openness at the heart of our work, we aim to meet each client with genuine care and thoughtful attention and attunement. Whatever brings you to therapy—whether it’s longstanding patterns, recent difficulties, or a search for clarity—you’ll find a place here where your experience can be spoken, explored, and understood.

People

Kevin Brohan, Director

I believe very deeply in the value of growth and change throughout our lives. Whether it is learning new ways of living and being, developing our creativity, broadening our experiences or changing how we relate to others, the underlying principle should be to develop our vitality.

I work with a broad range of issues, such as anxiety, depression, stress, grief, trauma and abuse. Whatever problems you are facing, I will work with you, with compassion, empathy and respect to build a therapeutic alliance. Our goal should be to find a solution to your difficulties in the shortest possible time.

Prior to becoming a therapist, I spent many years developing software of medical and scientific applications. I have an MSc in theoretical neuroscience and many year ago I completed a PhD on artificial intelligence.

You might come with a specific issue, or an awareness of being held back by unhelpful thinking, painful feelings or problematic behaviour. You might feel withdrawn and isolated, or you might feel your relationship with a loved one is intolerable, or that you are living a life that does not suit you, no matter how good it looks on paper or how impossible alternatives might appear now.

Talking things over with family or friends can help. But you might also feel like something in your relationships maintains your difficulties or you are not being truly heard. Or you may have reasons why that is not appropriate or why you need specialist help.

Then is the time to speak with an impartial professional, in confidence. I offer an initial online consultations free of charge, or you are very welcome to ask me a question. I mostly work weekday evenings.
