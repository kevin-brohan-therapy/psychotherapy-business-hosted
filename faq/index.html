Frequently Asked Questions

Here you’ll find answers to common questions about our therapy services, booking, and more.

How do I book a session?

You can contact us using the contact form or reach out directly to any therapist listed on the People page.

What types of therapy do you offer?

We offer a range of therapies including Transactional Analysis, integrative psychotherapy, and more. Please see the People page for therapist specializations.

Can I request a specific therapist?

Yes! Please mention your preferred therapist when you contact us.

Is online therapy available?

Yes, we offer both online and in-person sessions.

What are your fees?

Fees vary by therapist and session type. Please contact us for details.
